Emergency Dental Services in California

From knocked-out teeth to severe infections — here's what California emergency dentists can treat and what to expect.

Emergency dentists across California are equipped to handle a wide range of urgent dental conditions. Below is a complete guide to the most common emergency dental services, typical California cost ranges, and how urgently you should seek care.

Common emergency dental treatments

These are the procedures most frequently performed by California emergency dentists. Costs shown are California averages for patients without insurance.

🦷
Tooth Extraction
Removal of a severely damaged, decayed, or infected tooth. Simple extractions are quick and performed under local anesthetic. Surgical extractions are needed for broken or impacted teeth.
$150 – $500 (CA avg.)

Same day if painful
🔩
Emergency Root Canal
Required when infection reaches the tooth's inner pulp. Emergency root canals relieve severe pain immediately by removing infected tissue and sealing the tooth. Front teeth cost less than molars.
$700 – $1,500 (CA avg.)

Treat within 24 hrs
👑
Lost or Broken Crown
A lost crown leaves the tooth vulnerable to damage and sensitivity. Emergency dentists can recement existing crowns or place a temporary crown to protect the tooth until a permanent one is made.
$100 – $400 (CA avg.)

Within 1–2 days
💥
Broken or Chipped Tooth
Chips without nerve exposure can wait a day or two. Breaks exposing the nerve need immediate care. Treatment ranges from bonding and smoothing to full crowns depending on severity.
$150 – $1,200 (CA avg.)

Depends on severity
😷
Dental Abscess
A bacterial infection causing a pus pocket, severe throbbing pain, swelling, and sometimes fever. Abscesses can spread rapidly — this is a true emergency. Treatment includes drainage and antibiotics.
$200 – $600 (CA avg.)

Seek care today
⚡
Knocked-Out Tooth
A knocked-out permanent tooth can sometimes be reimplanted if treated within 30–60 minutes. Store the tooth in milk and call an emergency dentist immediately. Do not touch the root.
$200 – $800 (CA avg.)

Within 30–60 minutes
🪥
Emergency Filling
Lost or damaged fillings leave cavities exposed to bacteria, food, and pain. Emergency dentists can replace a filling the same day using composite resin or temporary filling material.
$150 – $400 (CA avg.)

Within 1–2 days
🩸
Gum Infection / Bleeding
Severe gum infections (periodontitis) can spread and lead to tooth loss or systemic infection. Emergency treatment includes scaling, root planing, and antibiotics to stop the infection.
$200 – $700 (CA avg.)

Treat within 24 hrs
🦴
Wisdom Tooth Pain
Impacted or infected wisdom teeth can cause extreme pain, swelling, and difficulty opening the mouth. Emergency dentists can prescribe antibiotics and perform extractions or refer to an oral surgeon.
$250 – $800 (CA avg.)

Within 24–48 hrs

How urgent is your dental emergency?

Call Now

Life-threatening — call 911

Difficulty breathing or swallowing from a dental infection, jaw fracture, uncontrolled bleeding, or severe facial trauma. Go to an ER immediately.

Same Day

Treat within hours

Knocked-out tooth (30–60 min window), dental abscess with swelling or fever, severe uncontrolled toothache, exposed nerve after a break.

24–48 Hours

Treat within a day or two

Lost crown or filling, cracked tooth without nerve exposure, broken dental work, moderate toothache, wisdom tooth pain without infection.

This Week

Routine urgent care

Small chips without pain, lost retainer, loose braces wire, mild sensitivity. These need attention but are not emergencies.
