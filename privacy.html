Privacy Policy

EmergencyDentistCA.com

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

EmergencyDentistCA.com ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website at emergencydentistca.com.

Information we collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Usage data: When you visit our site, we automatically collect information such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URL. This is collected through Google Analytics.
  • Search queries: When you use our Dentist Finder, we may log search terms (city, zip code) to improve our service. We do not collect or store personal health information.
  • Call tracking: If you click a phone number on our site, your call may be routed through a call-tracking service (e.g., CallRail or Ringba). These services may record that a call was made and its duration for business analytics purposes.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for analytics (Google Analytics) and advertising (Google AdSense). You can control cookies through your browser settings.

How we use your information

  • To operate and improve our website
  • To analyze traffic patterns and user behavior via Google Analytics
  • To serve relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To track referrals to dental providers for business analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects data such as pages viewed, session duration, and general geographic location (not your precise location). This data is aggregated and anonymized. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

Google AdSense

We may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other sites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings. We do not control the content of ads displayed by Google.

Pay-per-call advertising

Some phone numbers on this website are tracked call numbers provided by dental affiliates or call-tracking platforms. When you call these numbers, we may receive a referral fee. We are transparent about this business model and it does not affect the cost of any dental service to you.

Third-party links

Our website contains links to dental practice websites and Google Maps. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before sharing any personal information.

California residents — CCPA rights

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal data we collect, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal data. We do not sell personal data. To exercise your rights, contact us at the email below.

Children's privacy

Our site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Data retention

Analytics data is retained for up to 26 months per Google's default settings. Call tracking logs may be retained for up to 12 months. We do not retain personally identifiable health information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will post the updated version on this page with a revised "last updated" date. Your continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
